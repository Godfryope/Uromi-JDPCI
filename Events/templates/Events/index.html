About Us

Welcome to Uromi JDPCI

Uromi Justice Peace and Caritas Initiatives (Uromi JDPCI) extends a gracious welcome to you. As a faith-based Non-Governmental Organization affiliated with the esteemed Catholic Diocese of Uromi, our solemn mandate is to ardently promote Justice and Peace, encourage meaningful development, and steadfastly provide support for the less privileged across the distinguished 5 Local Government Areas of Edo Central Senatorial district.

In the noble pursuit of our mission, Uromi JDPCI is resolute in its commitment to making a substantial impact within our community. We invite you to join us in this noble endeavor, contributing to the creation of a more just and harmonious society.

Embarking on Community Development Initiatives

Promoting Justice and Peace with Diligence

Offering Meaningful Support to the Less Privileged

Fostering Collaboration with the Catholic Diocese of Uromi

Making a Positive Impact in the 5 Local Government Areas
